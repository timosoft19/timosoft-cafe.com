Position Title

MARKETING OFFICER (BIOVACCINES)

Description

The Marketing Officer will play a pivotal role in developing and implementing marketing and sales strategies for Biovaccines Nigeria Limited’s products. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of the pharmaceutical industry, excellent communication skills, and a proven track record in developing successful marketing campaigns. This role involves working closely with both private and public sectors to expand the reach and impact of our vaccine products.

Responsibilities

Marketing Strategy Development:
Key Responsibilities:

Marketing Strategy Development:

  •    Develop and execute comprehensive marketing plans to promote Biovaccines Nigeria Limited’s products in both private and public markets.
  •    Conduct market research to identify target audiences, market trends, and competitive landscape./li>
  •    Develop pricing strategies and promotional plans to maximize product reach and profitability.
Sales Plan Implementation:
  •    Create and implement sales plans to achieve organizational sales targets and revenue goals.
  •    Identify and engage with potential customers, including government health departments, hospitals, clinics, and private healthcare providers.
    • Brand Management:
  •    Assist in developing and implementing tax planning strategies to optimize the overall tax position of the pharmaceutical manufacturing company and its subsidiaries.
  •    Provide advice on the tax implications of business decisions.
    • Audit Support:
  •     Enhance the company’s brand presence through strategic marketing initiatives.
  •     Develop and oversee marketing materials, including brochures, digital content, and advertisements.
    • Public Relations and Communication:
  •     Act as a liaison between the company and the public, ensuring clear communication of the company’s mission, products, and achievements.
  •     Develop and maintain relationships with media outlets, healthcare organizations, and industry influencers.

Qualifications

  •    Bachelor’s degree in B. Pharm (preferred)/B.Sc. Biological Sciences (Microbiology, Biochemistry).
  •    Minimum of 3-5 years of experience in marketing, preferably in the pharmaceutical or healthcare industry.
  •    Strong understanding of the vaccine market and healthcare industry in Nigeria.
  • Excellent communication, negotiation, and interpersonal skills.
  •    Proficiency in digital marketing tools and techniques.
  •    Ability to work independently and as part of a team.
  •    Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.
  •     Proficient in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint).

Experience:

Minimum of four [4] years of relevant experience in tax accounting and subsidiary financial reporting.
